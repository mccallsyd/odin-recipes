Kale Salad Recipe

Description:

Bright and flavorful, a great side dish for pasta night.

Ingredients:

  • kale
  • pomegranante seeds
  • walnuts
  • vinegrette

Steps:

  1. Clean and trim kale leaves
  2. massage kale with dressing
  3. Top with walnuts and pomegranate seeds
  4. Serve and enjoy.
