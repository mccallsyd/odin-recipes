Poppy's Fried Fi'sh

Description:

Covered in seasoned breadcrumbs and deepfried, what more could you ask for?

Ingredients:

  • fi'sh
  • flour
  • just egg
  • bread crumbs
  • seasonings
  • oil

Steps:

  1. Preheat oil in wok to 350F
  2. mix flour and seasonings and place in bowl
  3. pour thin layer of just egg in shallow bowl
  4. pour breadcrumbs in shallow bowl
  5. dip fi'is in flour mix, then just egg, and finally breadcrumbs
  6. fry each fi'sh piece until golden brown
  7. Serve and enjoy.
